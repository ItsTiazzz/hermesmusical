Welcome to HERMES The Musical!

HERMES The Musical is a project based on Epic: The Musical, a nine-part series of album musicals written by Jorge Rivera-Herrans. The key difference? We want to give Hermes a bigger role, as a character who has a purpose closer to a commentator giving witty, sarcastic and mocking comments about the story, rather than just being able to watch the musical play out without interference.

This project is in its early stages, but you can follow along with us as we work on it. We will be posting updates and progress on our social media accounts, so be sure to follow us there! We are open to feedback and suggestions, so please don't hesitate to reach out to us. However, due to the nature of HPG, we cannot ask for much help with the project, we need to do most, if not all, of the work ourselves in order to maintain the integrity of the project. But like we said! Feedback is always welcome and much appreciated so we can improve the project as much as possible!
